# Holding Accounting

This context defines the product language for an accounting app focused on Norwegian holding companies.

## Language

**Holding-first accounting app**:
An accounting product designed around the recurring workflows of a Norwegian holding company before adding broader small-business accounting features.
_Avoid_: Fiken clone, generic accounting app

**Holding action**:
A holding-company event that changes ownership, investment value, owner distributions, intercompany balances, tax treatment, or annual compliance status.
_Avoid_: Feature, accounting task

**Regular holding company**:
A Norwegian holding company whose main activity is owning shares, receiving dividends, managing owner/company loans, paying basic administrative costs, and completing annual compliance.
_Avoid_: Operating company, management company

**Optional operating module**:
An accounting capability needed only when the company performs operating-business activities, such as invoicing customers, registering VAT-liable sales, or paying salary.
_Avoid_: Core holding feature, default feature

**Direct filing**:
Submission of statutory reports from the app to Norwegian public authorities or authority-backed filing channels, rather than merely preparing numbers for manual filing elsewhere.
_Avoid_: Preparation-only, export pack

**Annual holding compliance loop**:
The recurring statutory reporting cycle a regular Norwegian holding company must complete each year: shareholder register statement, company tax return, and annual accounts.
_Avoid_: Full compliance, all filings

**Owner-managed direct filing**:
A filing model where the company owner or legal representative authenticates, reviews, confirms, and submits statutory reports from the app.
_Avoid_: Accountant-mediated filing

**Simple holding AS**:
A regular holding company with one bank account, few yearly transactions, investments in companies or securities, no payroll, no VAT, and no customer invoicing.
_Avoid_: SME, operating business

**Transaction capture**:
The way bank and cash movements enter the accounting workflow before they become posted ledger entries.
_Avoid_: Bank integration

**Holding-action wizard**:
A guided workflow that asks about a holding action in ordinary company-owner language and generates reviewable accounting entries from deterministic rules.
_Avoid_: Manual journal first

**Filing readiness gate**:
The required validation checkpoint before direct filing that blocks submission until hard errors are resolved and warnings are explicitly accepted by the authorized user.
_Avoid_: Final checklist

**Filing simulation**:
A non-submitting filing workflow that builds the intended filing data, validates known rules, shows the same user preview expected for direct filing, and stores a simulated receipt.
_Avoid_: Mock filing, fake filing

**Launch promise**:
The public scope commitment that the app directly files the annual reports for a simple Norwegian holding AS and blocks unsupported cases.
_Avoid_: Vision, roadmap

**Parallel-run validation**:
A pre-launch validation process where filing simulation outputs are compared against Fiken, accountant-prepared, or previously submitted filings for real simple holding companies.
_Avoid_: Manual QA

**Public-data validation**:
A validation layer using public annual accounts, public shareholder information where available, official examples, and synthetic golden cases before testing with private company data.
_Avoid_: Production validation

**Filing assistant with narrow ledger**:
A product category where the app maintains only the ledger needed for supported holding actions so it can prepare and directly file the annual holding compliance loop.
_Avoid_: Full accounting system

**Narrow ledger**:
A double-entry ledger limited to the accounts and postings required for simple holding-company actions and annual filings.
_Avoid_: General ledger

**Structured entry**:
A ledger entry generated by a holding-action wizard with known intent and deterministic filing implications.
_Avoid_: Normal entry

**Unstructured entry**:
A manual journal entry whose business intent is not fully known to the app and may reduce filing confidence.
_Avoid_: Advanced entry

**Simple security investment**:
A manually maintained listed share or fund position treated as a holding investment with low transaction volume.
_Avoid_: Trading portfolio

**Templated corporate document**:
A standardized company document generated from supported annual close or dividend data without providing custom legal advice.
_Avoid_: Legal document automation

**Accounting document**:
A stored source or support document needed to explain, verify, file, or later audit a holding-company action or annual filing.
_Avoid_: Attachment

**Company archive**:
An exportable collection of the company’s ledger, accounting documents, generated corporate documents, filing previews, and submission receipts for a selected period.
_Avoid_: Backup

**Optional accountant review**:
A paid or invited review workflow where an accountant can inspect records and filings before submission, without being required for simple holding-company filings.
_Avoid_: Mandatory accountant approval

**Needs-accountant case**:
A company situation or transaction pattern that falls outside the simple holding AS path and should be blocked, escalated, or strongly warned before owner-managed direct filing.
_Avoid_: Edge case

**Risk response**:
The filing behavior triggered by a validation or support-boundary issue: hard block, escalation required, or explicit warning.
_Avoid_: Error handling

**New-year start**:
An onboarding path where the company begins using the app from a chosen accounting year with opening balances, imported bank transactions, investment/shareholder setup, and prior annual accounts as reference.
_Avoid_: Full migration

**Founder pricing**:
A lower lifetime price offered to a limited early cohort to compensate for adopting a new and initially less trusted filing product.
_Avoid_: Permanent discount

**Year-end interview**:
A guided annual close workflow that asks owner-level questions and maps answers to ledger, shareholder, tax, and annual-account fields.
_Avoid_: Filing form

**Norwegian-first product language**:
The product language policy where user-facing terms, workflows, and filing explanations default to Norwegian authority and accounting terminology.
_Avoid_: English-first UI

**Talli**:
The working brand for the holding-first filing assistant.
_Avoid_: Holdingklar

**Single-company UX**:
A user experience optimized for an owner working on one company at a time, even though the underlying account can own or access multiple companies.
_Avoid_: Accountant practice UI
